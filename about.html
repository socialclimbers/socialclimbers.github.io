About Social Climbers

Social Climbers is a specialist climbing shop and service based locally and managed by John "Lofty" Thomas. We provide climbing gear, guided trips, coaching and safety workshops for climbers and adventurers.

Our mission

We help climbers of all backgrounds progress safely and sustainably — from first climbs at the indoor wall to technical mountain routes. We prioritise safety, community and skill development.
